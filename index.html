Recommendded Cafe in Ipoh

PLan B

In terms of size and level of hipster-ness:
This place matches the cafe standards of Kuala Lumpur.
There’s a lot of room inside and outdoors.
And the design of the cafe features a mixture of industrial and grunge elements.

Want to pick up some souvenirs?
There are plenty of quaint shops in the vicinity of Plan B (Kong Heng Square).
Ipoh Concubine Lane is just opposite, too!

Opening Hours: Sunday to Thursday: 11am–10pm
Friday to Saturday: 11am–10:30pm
Address: No. 75, Jalan Panglima, 30000 Ipoh, Negeri Perak, Malaysia
Contact: +605-249 8286
Cuisine: Fusion of Western and Asian cuisine
Remarks:
Prices are pretty steep compared to the other cafes on the list. Vegetarian Friendly, Vegan Options, Halal They do serve liquor here



Food & Desire Café

Recently moved, Food & Desire Café is now open for private dining and takeaways only. So make a reservation before you go!

Note: It costs ~RM30 per meal
It’s worth the trouble too as this establishment prioritizes on the quality of their food! You can see that food is truly their passion from the menu!
Depending on availability, they even have quiche, bento boxes, chili crabs, and roast pork belly!

Opening Hours: Daily 12 pm – 3:30 pm and again from 6 – 9pm (Closed on Monday)
Address: 2, Jalan Chew Peng Loon, Ipoh Garden
Contact: +6012 502 5540
Cuisine: Western
Remarks:
They sell New Zealand ice cream (limited flavors) Their specialties are private parties and pork burgers (they have Siew Yoke pasta too) Wide tea selection Coffee is good



Ninety One

You can see this cafe as you drive along Jalan Raja Dihilir.
The large windows add to the sense of space inside the cafe.
This is an excellent place to meet friends or have birthday parties

Some of their drinks come with milk cubes.

Opening Hours: Daily from 10 am – 4:30 pm (Closed on Mondays and Tuesdays)
Address: 91, Jalan Raja Dihilir, Ipoh, 30350 Ipoh, Negeri Perak
Contact: +6011-5962 9019
Cuisine: Some Japanese, some western. Desserts available. They have some unique cake flavors.
Remarks:
Delectable coffee to be had here. Fantastic ambiance Has its own parking slots for customers unlike many of the cafes in Ipoh. If you like large portions of food, you probably won’t get them here.
